E-Voting & Election Management System

E-Voting system based on Java Spring Boot

Accomplishments
  • Tools & Tech: Java, Spring Boot, ReactJs, MaterialUI, MongoDB, VS Code, Intellij Idea

  • By using our platform, users no longer need to travel extensively to their designated constituency in order to cast their vote. Instead, they can visit any voting booth across the country and exercise their voting rights from there, regardless of their registered constituency.
